Quest 4 Papa

Quest 4 Papa is a 3D bullet-hell top down shooter. Made with Unreal Engine 4.

You play as Roach, a girl who makes a living by getting the pieces of old abandoned robots.

Robots will have all sorts of guns and attack any strager who approaches them.

My role as Tools Programmer

During the development of Quest 4 Papa I focused on tools to make the game scope approchable. Working with artists, programmers and designers in the creation of dungeons, rooms and its interaction with the enemies. Here below are some of my main tasks. (This project is work in progress)

  • UE4 Dugeon Creator Plugin

    It creates a dungeon from a given image, following a color coding. You can specify which objects to place as walls, doors, ground etc. It will also use dynamic meshes to merge all floor tiles into a single mesh. (Same for roofs). After that, you can drag enemies in the rooms and everything will connect appropietly without any manual work.

  • Minimap and full size map

    Displays on the HUD the minimap of the dungeon and the icons of the wanted characters in real time. This is done by just adding a component to the wanted actor. It can also show the fullmap view.

  • Shaders and graphical effects

    I was in charge of the creation of some of the effects like opening the roofs when a player enters a room. Some post-processing like the outline shader. And general shader materials like the one used by the bullets in the game.
